Help
How may we help you, our client
Our Client

Our client, this is our help center, where you can find the answers to some or all the questions you might have. We take our clients queries very seriously, make sure we attend to them at our very best.


Our client, you will see a list of queries which you might have. Also, be aware that we will be updating this regulary. If you see anyone of them that matches your query, just click on the question and it display the answer to that question. Below is the list of queries.

Where is my product Id?

Your Product Id

Your product Id is in you account section. Go to the top-right of this page. Click on the user icon, you will see a dropdown menu. click on 'MY ACCOUNT' link. that will take you to your account and inside your account section, you will see a window labeled 'My Products'. You can scroll down this window to get the Id of any product you might have purchased from us. Note: each product you purchase has an Id.

Where is my service Id?

Your Service Id

Your service Id is in you account section. Go to the top-right of this page. Click on the user icon, you will see a dropdown menu. click on 'MY ACCOUNT' link. that will take you to your account and inside your account section, you will see a window labeled 'My Services'. You can scroll down this window to get the Id of any service we are providing you. Note: each service we provide you has an Id.

Where is my contract Id?

Your Contract Id

Your contract Id is in you account section. Go to the top-right of this page. Click on the user icon, you will see a dropdown menu. click on 'MY ACCOUNT' link. that will take you to your account and inside your account section, you will see a window labeled 'My Contracts'. You can scroll down this window to get the Id of any contract you gave us to execute for you. Note: each contract we execute for you has an Id.

How do I pay for my product?

Payment for Product

You can pay for the product you purchase from us online via paypal, direct debit (debit/credit card) or even by bank. For the latter, we will give you our details where you can make the payment or you write us a check.

How do I pay for my service?

Payment for Service

You can pay for the service we provide you monthly online via paypal, direct debit (debit/credit card) or even by bank. For the latter, we will give you our details where you can make the payment or you write us a check.

How do I pay for my contract?

Payment for Contract

You can pay for the contract we execute for you online via paypal, direct debit (debit/credit card) or even by bank. For the latter, we will give you our details where you can make the payment or you write us a check. Note: the payment for the contract can be made when you (our client) and us agree on price for the contract.

How do I report problems about my product?

Problems about your product

You can report problems about your product by going to the support section and telling us what is wrong with your product so that we can fix it. You can also report by calling the number right at the top of this page or by using the contact section.

How do I report problems about my service?

Problems about your service

You can report problems about your service by going to the support section and telling us what is wrong with your service so that we can fix it. You can also report by calling the number right at the top of this page or by using the contact section.

How do I report problems about my contract?

Problems about your contract

You can report problems about your contract by going to the support section and telling us what is wrong with your contract so that we can fix it. You can also report by calling the number right at the top of this page or by using the contact section.

Do I need to register to pay for or access the product, service or contract?

Whether you need to register to pay

You do not need to register to pay for any of this. You can login using our third-party client login.

Can I request for a money back?

Request for money back

Of course, you are entitled to money back within one week after payment or purchase.

Is my information well secured?

Security of your data

Your information is well secured. We take the protection of our client data very seriously. Your data will also not be given to any third party without your consent.

Can I revoke or cancel my service or contract?

Revoke of contract or service

You can revoke your contract or service by clicking on the bin tag besides that particular contract or service in the contract or service mini window in your account section. Note: you cannot cancel a contract/service once it is finished. You can only cancel it within seven days of its registeration. Once it is finished you can only recycle the detail history.

Can I have an account without registering?

Account without registration

You can have an account (guest account) without registering with us when you use a third-party client login to purchase or use our resources.

Can I cancel my account whenever I wish?

Account Revoking

You can cancel your account anytime you wish to do so by clicking on the recycle bin in the top-left corner of the account section.

Where do my information go when I cancel my account?

What happens to your data when you cancel

Your information is removed completely from our system when you cancel your account.

Do I avail of any offer?

Offer

You avail of offers when they come up. We will send you a notification email about the offer.

Are details of my transactions saved?

Receipt

Each transactions you perform comes with a receipt. These receipts appear in your account with Jend Solutions and also are emailed to you.

How, where and when do I get the product I purchased?

Products

When you purchase a product from us it will be available for download on your next login in the your account download section. In addition, we also email you a copy for download.

How, where and when do I get the service I paid for?

Service

When you pay for a service the service will be provided for you from our server. e.g say, you pay for database service, we will develop and provide this database service from our server thereby allowing you to use our web service to access your database any where you are.

How, where and when do I get the contract I paid for?

Contract

When you pay for a contract we execute and implement the solution where ever you want it e.g your company, home, office etc.

Where do I see my transaction history?

History

For service and contracts, the transaction history can be found in mini windows on service and contract in your account section. For purchased products, it can be found by clicking on the download history link in the asset menu in your account section.
